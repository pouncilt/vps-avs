Clinical Charts

AVS Sections ››

Clinical charts may be optionally included in the AVS.  The charts are all line graphs and reflect the lab data and vital signs over the past year for the patient.  Clinical charts that are available include BMI, BP, Cholesterol, Creatinine, eGFR, HbA1C, HDL, HGB, LDL, Platelets, Pulse, Triglycerides, and Weight.
